# References

## Sample app

Welcome to the world of feature flagging made effortless with the ConfigCat-Vue plugin! Our sample Vue.js app serves as a gateway to exploring the seamless integration and dynamic configuration capabilities that ConfigCat brings to your Vue.js projects. With the ConfigCat-Vue plugin, you can easily control the rollout of features, A/B test your application, and manage configurations remotely without the need for code changes. This introductory sample app showcases the simplicity and power of ConfigCat's feature management platform, empowering developers to deliver personalized and tailored experiences to their users like never before. Let's embark on this journey to unlock the true potential of feature flagging with Vue.js and ConfigCat!
